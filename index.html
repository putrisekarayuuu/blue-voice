Blue Voice

When Data Drives Sustainability

Blue Voice is a sentiment analysis project that supports sustainable coastal tourism by leveraging AI and data science to assess visitor experiences through online reviews. Enabling smarter, more sustainable decision-making through data.

Learn more

Project Brief Description

We focus specifically on sustainable tourism in coastal areas because these destinations often face fragile environmental and socio-cultural pressures. Managing growth while preserving ecological integrity requires not just policies — but also real-time insights from those who experience these places firsthand.

What Data Do We Use?

Blue Voice processes user-generated reviews from travel platforms such as TripAdvisor and Google Maps. The data includes:

  • Visitor reviews and ratings
  • Keywords and phrases that reflect opinions on attractions, amenities, pricing, access, and more
  • Temporal and spatial patterns — when and where sentiments shift

All data is ethically collected, anonymized, and used for non-commercial, research-oriented purposes.

Why Focus on Sustainable Coastal Tourism?

Coastal destinations are among the most visited — and most vulnerable — regions globally. They face challenges such as:

  • Overtourism and overcrowding
  • Environmental degradation (e.g., coral reef damage, erosion, pollution)
  • Pressure on local communities and infrastructure

Yet, many of these areas lack access to actionable data that could help them monitor and manage tourism more sustainably. Sentiment analysis helps surface early indicators of visitor satisfaction or concern — enabling proactive decisions.

Our Mission

Blue Voice empowers local governments, destination managers, and communities with:

  • Data-driven insights on how tourists feel
  • Dashboards to visualize sentiment trends

With Blue Voice, we’re not just analyzing text — we’re amplifying the collective voice of travelers for the betterment of people, place, and planet.

Enabling smarter, more sustainable decision-making through data.
